Frontend Foxes Homework - Shopping Cart Data Types

Understanding what shopping cart is?

  • A piece of software
  • Keeps the record of the items a buyer intends to buy (or 'picks up') from the online store. In a pysical store, it means putting the items in the basket you carry around the shop.
  • Acts as an online store's catalog and the cart should enable customers to do the following:
    1. Select products
    2. Review what they have selected
    3. Make modifications
    4. Add extra items if needed
    5. Purchase the Products (Make payment)

Example of shopping carts:

  • Hosted shopping carts e.g Shopify
  • Self hosted shopping carts e.g Magento, WooCommerce (Plugin for wordpress)
  • Simple shopping carts e.g Paypal Cart

A User Story

A user named Jane wants to buy a new baby toy on an online store. She should be able to see:

  • A storefront to see the products
  • Stock availability
  • Name and description of product
  • Listed price
  • Ability to add Item to basket
  • To go to the basket, review what she selected
  • Add more items
  • Make payment
